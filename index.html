Hi There!
I'm Sid

Hey there, I'm Sid, your go-to scuba dive master here in the paradise of Koh Tao, Thailand! With a perpetual grin and a passion for exploration, I've led countless dives into the mesmerizing depths of these turquoise waters. From playful clownfish to graceful manta rays, I've had the pleasure of befriending the diverse marine life that calls this place home. So, grab your gear and let's dive into an underwater adventure where every flipper kick brings a new thrill and every dive is an unforgettable experience!

contact me at hello@scubasid.com
